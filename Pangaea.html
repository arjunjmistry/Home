Festival: Pangaea Music & Arts

Theme: Disco Apocalypse

Location: Manchester

Year: 2014

Dj booth utilising a matrix of fluorescent white string to create a 3-dimensional parabolic ‘wormhole’ illuminated by two UV cannons. CNC plywood outer structure with translucent polypropylene cladding in pentagon geometry, providing protection from rain.

Pangaea was a music and arts Festival originally starting in 2006 which grew to become the largest student festival in Europe. Between 2014-2016 Arjun project managed the architectural decor team to design, resource and fabricate a series of indoor and outdoor architectural installations, responding to each unique festival theme.
