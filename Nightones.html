Artist: pet Deaths

Installation Type: Video

Graphic Design: Andrew Hobbs

Year: 2021

Nightones is an open ended exploration into liminal experiences between wakefulness and sleep. Collaborating with artists Pet Deaths for the second instalment, the installation for Nightones ii was envisaged as an online tv channel transmitting sounds and images from the movie playing in your mind moments before you fall asleep.

Accompanying a soundtrack structured around loose improvisations, audio cuts and pieces assembled organically through the 2020 lockdown, is a visualiser of archival footage coalesced into a narratives which explore themes of lightness, dissonance, narcosis, somnolence, hypnic convulsions and blissful ascension.

The project was funded through a limited release of 50 tapes which allowed exclusive access to Nightones TV.
