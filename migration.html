Festival: Lewes Light

Theme: Moon & Water

Budget: £600

Year: 2020

Migration is an outdoor art installation commissioned by Lewes Light Festival, utilising escher-esque retroreflective tessellations to form a monochrome backdrop illuminated by LED projectors.

At key moments, when the visitor’s viewing angle align parallel with the direction of the light source, the retroreflective tiles dramatically reflect the light back and appear as a self-illuminating surface.

Oriented South, the retroreflective effect could be experienced off of the Sun’s reflection, for an installation that could be experienced during day and night.
