Festival: Amsterdam Light Festival

Theme: Positive Disruption

Location: Amsterdam

Completion: Unrealised

Moiré is an unrealised art installation, responding to Amsterdam Light Festival’s theme for positive disruption.

Spatial interference patterns flourish from the simple rotating motion of two green LED gobo projectors. At a macro scale, individual constructive and destructive interferences of each illuminating cell may not appear significant, but at a larger visual scale, patterns of movement emerge and manifest.

Inspiration for this installation was drawn from the Inogon Leader Marks which utilises this visual illusion to guide ships back to the dock.
