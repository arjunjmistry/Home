Product: Lamp

Reflections: Infinite

Year: 2018

Infinity Lamp is an attempt to domesticate the visual phenomena that arises when a glass mirror reflects the image in an opposing glass mirror, transmitting some of the light with each reflection until the image of the light source seemingly evaporates into the distance.

The geometry is kept simple with a warm white opalescent, spherical LED bulb placed at the centre of a black aluminium framed glass cube with internal mirrored films applied, fixed to a wooden base. In this way, the table lamp is imagined to suit the material palette of contemporary apartment interiors.
